The Art Of the Neapolitan Soul

A Once-a-Year Culinary Pilgrimage to the Heart of Pizza. Master the ancient techniques from the legendary masters of Naples.

THE JOURNEY

Seven Days of Sacred Dough

"To make pizza is to handle life itself. In the humid air of Naples, where the shadow of Vesuvius touches the stone, we teach more than recipes. We teach the rhythm of the fermentation, the language of the flame, and the respect for the soil."

Our 7-day immersion is limited to twelve students annually, ensuring intimate hands-on mastery in a private historical kitchen overlooking the Mediterranean.
